Reviewer Roles#

This review was conducted from five perspectives: Senior Historian, Academic Peer Reviewer,
National History Day Judge, AP U.S. History Reader, and Historical Archivist. It audits the completed knowledge base (harriet_tubman_primary_sources.md and harriet_tubman_research.md), documents every omission and correction, and records the enhancements applied.

Review Method#

Each topic, source, and claim in the existing files was checked against the standard of primary-source verifiability. Where reliable evidence existed, the gap was filled and the source files updated; where no reliable source exists, the gap is flagged as unresolved. A claim-by-claim confidence audit follows.
