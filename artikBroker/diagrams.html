① In plain English

artikBroker is a website where you type in stock symbols (like NVDA) — or just describe what you're looking for in normal words — and it gives each stock a score from 0 to 100 with a clear BUY / HOLD / SELL verdict and the reasons behind it.

The two ways to use it

  • Type stock symbols → it instantly analyzes those exact companies.
  • Type a question (e.g. "cheap energy stocks that aren't overbought") → an AI assistant figures out which companies you might mean, then the app scores them.

Where the numbers come from

  • Live company financials are pulled from Yahoo Finance (free, public market data).
  • A consistent scoring engine grades every company the same way — value, quality, growth, financial strength, momentum, and risk — so the verdicts are comparable.
  • The AI only helps interpret your words and suggest companies. It never makes up the scores — the engine always does the math on real data.
A simple analogy. Think of it like a restaurant. You (the customer) place an order at the counter (the website). A translator (the AI) helps if you describe what you're craving instead of naming a dish. The kitchen (the scoring engine) cooks every dish to the exact same recipe using fresh ingredients (Yahoo Finance data). You always get a consistent, comparable plate back — the verdict and the reasons.

Is it secure?

  • The site is password-protected — you log in before you can use it.
  • All sensitive keys (AI accounts, passwords) are stored in a locked AWS vault, never in the code.

The one thing to remember: the AI only understands your question and suggests companies (Step 1). The real scores always come from the engine using live data (Step 2). That keeps results consistent and trustworthy, no matter how you phrased the search.
